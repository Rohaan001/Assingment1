Biryani

ingrideints of biryani

  • 1 cup rice
  • 1/2 cup chicken
  • 1/2 cup onions
  • 1/2 cup tomato
  • 1/2 cup masala
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1/2 cup salt
  • 1/2 cup sugar
    1. 1 cup boiled basmati rice
    2. 1/2 teaspoon mint leaves
    3. 1/2 teaspoon fenugreek seeds
    4. 1/2 teaspoon curry powder
    5. 1/2 teaspoon turmeric
    6. 1/2 teaspoon coriander powder
    7. 1/2 teaspoon cumin seeds
    8. 1/2 teaspoon red chilli powder
    9. 1/2 teaspoon black pepper

    Directions

  • If you want your chicken to be juicier in your biryani, do not remove the bone.
  • The restaurant-style chicken biryani recipe uses the whole chicken in preparation and the chicken can dry when cooking at home. Always use chicken thigh or drumsticks
  • The first and foremost important thing to take care of while preparing chicken biryani recipe is, always use a heavy-bottomed pan as you would not want the chicken getting cooked.
  • Always use oil
  • Always use salt
  • Always use water
  • Always use red chilli powder
  • Always use turmeric powder
  • Always use fenugreek seeds
  • Always use curry powder
