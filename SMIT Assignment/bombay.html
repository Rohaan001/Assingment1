Bombay Biryani

Description

Shan Bombay Biryani mix helps you recreate the authentic traditional taste of Bombay Biryani which is a true legacy of the sub-continental cuisine.

Information

Ingredients

  • 1 cup water
  • 1/2 cup plain flour
  • 1/4 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1/4 tsp cumin powder
  • 1/4 tsp red chilli powder
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper
  • 1/4 tsp coriander powder
  • 1/4 tsp garam masala powder
  • 1/4 tsp fenugreek seeds
  • 1/4 tsp fenugreek powder

Allergy Information

May Contain: Traces Of Sulphite, Sesame, Mustard, Gluten And Tree Nuts.

Storage Condition

Store In A Cool, Dry Place.

Brand Message

Bombay Biryani is a true legacy of the sub-continental cuisine.
